Stretch to release tension.

Hold each position for 15 seconds.

Slowly relax your torso and bend over at the waist, reaching your fingertips towards your toes.
Look upwards as you extend your arms up and behind you, arching your back and letting the weight of your upper body push against your hips.
With your shoulders and hips facing the same direction, spread your legs apart and bend to the side at the waist. Reach your upper arm straight out to the side. Repeat on opposite side.
Lay flat on your stomach with your chest to the ground and your hands on either end of your torso, as if you were about to do a pushup. Then slowly push your hands against the ground, locking your elbows and completely relaxing your upper torso while looking upwards at the ceiling.
While still bent at the waist with your hands hanging downwards, clasp them behind your back and stretch them out behind you and towards the ground.
Keeping your hands together above your head, align your shoulders and hips while bending at the waist to the side, focusing on staying aligned. Repeat on opposite side.
Grasp your right ankle and pull it behind you, balancing on your other foot. Raise your left hand in the air and hold position. Repeat on opposite side.
Release your suspended position and relax on the ground. Now push backwards instead with your arms, and let your lower body raise to the air. Hold this position.
