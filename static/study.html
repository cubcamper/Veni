Study Tips

So you think you're doing everything right, but you still aren't getting the results you want... Why not? Find out here, where we give you some tips and tricks to study better.

Start in the classroom.

  • Start by reading the assignment before the lecture. Sit where you can see the presentation and hear the the professor/teacher. Schedule your study time by writing down everything you need to do and allotting adequate amounts of time to complete the test.
  • Attend all classes/lectures. Write lecture notes in class and rewrite/review them when you get home to help you remember them. Make sure to write neatly sometimes you will not have time to rewrite the notes. Highlight with discretion while reviewing your notes.
  • Ask questions if you are confused with a concept, make sure to identify the exact step where you get confused.
  • Make sure to write information down even if you think that you are going to remember the information.

Stay organized!

  • Understand the importance of time management. Take advantage of our Pomodoro timing system!
  • Make sure to have a organized study place. Make sure to identify what type of learner you are (and study appropriately). Make sure to actively read when going through gm Make sure to meditate daily.
  • If you feel like you are not aware of where all the time is going, then with a watch, pencil and piece of paper record what you are doing throughout the course of the day and how much time you spend for each activity.
  • Plan the things you need to do tomorrow before going to sleep. Make a weekly to-do list followed by a to-do list everyday. Assign each tak an estimated amount of time that it'll time to complete the assignment. Study after you have scheduled all of your obligations. Make sure to sleep at-least 7 hours a day, sleep is the time when the brain makes/rewires neural connections. If have not been getting adequate amounts of sleep before a major test, sleep at-least 8 hours for the two days leading up to the major test. Make sure to eat something the day of the test (don't eat too much, and make sure not to change your diet on the day of the test).
